  • What will it cost me to use SnipBills?

    SnipBills does not charge merchants any signup or monthly subscription fees, nor do you require any additional hardware to use the service. The only cost involved with using SnipBills is a 8% transaction fee (incl. VAT) charged to the merchant.

  • How do I know whether a customer’s payment has been successful?

    Every time a customer successfully pay using SnipeBills, you will immediately receive payment confirmation via SMS.

  • What if I don’t receive an SMS or other form of notification?

  • My customers are using SnipeBills to pay, but the funds are not reflecting in my account.

    SnipeBills settles transaction amounts using a daily settlement system. At midnight each day, all transactions from the previous 24 hour period are tallied and transferred directly into your bank account.
